How I rebuilt a brand's entire communications rhythm in 3 months

American Women In Tech had the audience but their messaging was scattered across channels. I came to fix that.

Role Comms Talent
Focus Social + Corporate Communications
Timeline 2024
Region USA

Challenge

The brand had a growing audience but no consistency in how they showed up. Social media, newsletters, and corporate communications were all running on different tracks. The voice changed depending on the channel, and engagement was dropping because of it.

Approach

  • I took over the social accounts and rebuilt the content calendar from scratch.
  • Rewrote the newsletter format to make it something people actually opened.
  • Cleaned up the corporate communications so that every email, every post, every update sounded like it came from the same brand.
  • Then I set up a system so it could run smoothly after I left.

Results

  • Follower engagement up 65%
  • Newsletter open rates improved by 42%
  • Consistent brand voice across all digital channels
