Case Study

Thrive Financials

In 2023, I led content and brand management for a financial education platform, aligning strategy, channels, and campaign goals to measurable growth outcomes.

Role Content & Brand Manager
Focus Strategy + Campaign Execution
Timeline 2023
Region Remote

Challenge

The brand needed to improve consistency, grow audience quality, and turn educational content into stronger enrollment and event outcomes.

Approach

  • Built an end-to-end social media strategy tied to clear KPI targets.
  • Produced educational content for budgeting, investing, credit, and retirement topics.
  • Coordinated content across social media, email marketing, and web channels.

Results

  • Grew social media following by 74% in two months.
  • Increased course enrollments and event registrations by 400%.
  • Improved brand awareness and trust positioning in the category.
