Case Study

How a financial literacy platform went from invisible to fully booked

Thrive Financials had great educational content but no system to get it in front of the right people. I built one.

Role Content & Brand Manager
Focus Content Strategy + Community Growth
Timeline 2023
Region Nigeria

Challenge

Thrive Financials was producing solid educational content on budgeting, saving, and investing, but barely anyone was seeing it. Course enrollments were low, event attendance was inconsistent, and the brand didn't have a clear identity that set it apart from other financial education platforms.

Approach

  • I built a content strategy tied to their actual business goals, not just posting for the sake of posting.
  • Developed a social media plan that drove people toward courses and events.
  • Created content across social, email, and the website that all pointed in the same direction.
  • Positioned them as a trusted voice in financial literacy, not just another page sharing money tips.

Results

  • Social media following grew 74% in 2 months
  • Course enrollments and event registrations up 400%
  • Established brand positioning as a trusted financial education resource
