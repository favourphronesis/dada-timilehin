How I Work

I don't start with tactics. I start with understanding.

01

Listen & Audit

Before I touch anything, I dig into what's already there. Your existing content, your audience data, your brand voice, what's working, what's not.
Most brands have more signal than they think. They just haven't read it properly yet.

02

Strategy & Positioning

I map out who you're talking to, what they care about, and where the gaps are between what you're saying and what they need to hear.
Once that's clear, every decision after it becomes obvious.

03

Build & Execute

Content, campaigns, community, partnerships. Whatever the strategy calls for, I build it and run it across channels. A blog post feeds a newsletter, a newsletter feeds social, social feeds community.

04

Measure & Adjust

I track what matters. Not vanity numbers, but the metrics tied to your actual goals. Subscriber growth, engagement quality, traffic, conversions.
Then I adjust based on what the data says, not what looks good on a report.

Why Work With Me

I got into marketing because I believed African businesses deserved to be seen on a global stage. That was the conviction before I had any skills to back it up. Now I have both.

Over four years, I've worked with companies across Lagos, the US, the Netherlands, and remote teams everywhere in between. What I've noticed is that the best African brands often have the hardest time getting noticed. Not because the work isn't good. Because the storytelling, the positioning, the systems around visibility just aren't there yet.

That's the gap I fill.

I've sat in the weeds of Web3 communities, fintech launches, nonprofit campaigns, and entertainment brands. I've done the scrappy early-stage work and the structured corporate strategy. That range isn't something I stumbled into. It's what happens when you care more about the outcome than the job title.

If you're building something worth paying attention to, I'll make sure people actually pay attention.
