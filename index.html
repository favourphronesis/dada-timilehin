About Me

Before I knew what "startup" even meant, I knew I wanted African businesses to be seen globally. That conviction hasn't changed. Marketing just became the tool I picked up to make it happen.

Over the past four years, I've worked across fintech, HRtech, Web3, lifestyle, and entertainment. I've built brands from the ground up, launched tokens, scaled communities, and run campaigns across multiple countries. Some of my work has been local. Some of it has reached audiences in the US, Netherlands, and beyond.

I see brands as living stories. My job is to figure out what story a company is trying to tell, then make sure the right people hear it. Through strategy, content, PR, and campaigns that tie back to real numbers.

The work has always been the same at its core: help brilliant companies stop being invisible.
